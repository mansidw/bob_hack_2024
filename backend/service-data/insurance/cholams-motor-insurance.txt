This could be the owner, passengers, driver, pedestrians, etc.Damage to property of others - also called as Third Party Property DamagePartial damage to the vehicle itself. This may arise due to accidental external means, riot, strike, malicious act, fire, flood, cyclone, etc.Theft of parts or theft of the vehicle itself.Total loss/damage of the vehicle.Compulsory Personal Accident (CPA) cover for Owner-driverThe CPA cover offers compensation up to Rs.15 lakhs for Accidental Death/Disability of the registered Owner of the Insured Vehicle whilst driving or alighting into/out of the Vehicle.The registered Owner should hold an effective driving license.Key requirements for acceptanceProposal formValuation report (where applicable)Renewal notice copy (in case of an insurance renewal)Break in insurance iInspection report if there is a break in insurance or there is no past insurance.Copy of RC book/iInvoice Copy (where applicable)Claims ProcedureInform CholaMS customer care on a toll free number 1800 5700 and register the claim.Please provide policy number, driver details, incident details, location of accident, details of damages etc. to the customer care executiveSafeguard self and try to prevent further damage to the vehicle by taking appropriate action.In case of a 2 of 2 wheeler, there is no need for a spot for spot survey. It is mandatory under the Motor Vehicles Act for all vehicles to have at least 'Third Party Liability' coverage. Chola MS motor insurance offers a range of motor insurance products for private cars, two-wheelers, commercial vehicles, trailers, ambulances, tractors, and other special types of vehicles. Therefore,it is important to safeguard yourself and your vehicle with CholaMS motor insurance.	What are the key benefits of Cholamandalam Motor Insurance?The Chola car insurance offers numerous benefitsComprehensive policy covering both private and commercial vehicles, including miscellaneous classes (own damage and third-party liability)Unique add-on covers available for additional premium with unlimited claimsInsuring uninsured vehicles based on online submission of vehicle photosRoadside assistance services offered nationwide at a nominal premiumCustomizable coverage options to suit individual needsStrong presence in Tier 2/3/4 cities for wider market reachLive video streaming process for vehicle surveysConvenient policy renewal through mobile appDedicated staff to handle policy renewalsMultiple payment options available, including cheque, ACH, NEFT, and debit/credit card with payment gateway access.	What is the claim process for CholaMS Motor Insurance?Claim intimation for the Chola motor insurance must be given to the insurer as per policy conditions.It can be done through regional or branch offices, agents, intermediaries, or CRM via phone or email.Various details are recorded, including insured's name, address, contact details, policy number, date and cause of loss, location, description of loss, estimate of loss, and hospital details if applicable.Upon receiving the information, policy validity and coverage details are verified.If the claim is admissible, it is registered.A surveyor or in-house loss assessor conducts the survey within specified timeframes, either physically or through digital means.The assessment basis is explained to the insured or
