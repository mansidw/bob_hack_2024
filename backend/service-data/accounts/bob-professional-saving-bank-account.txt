It is designed to cater to the banking needs of professionals such as doctors, engineers, architects, chartered accountants, lawyers, and other self-employed individuals. This account provides various benefits like higher transaction limits, personalised cheque books, and access to a range of banking services.Who is eligible to open a bob Professional Saving Bank Account?The Baroda Professional Saving Bank Account is available to qualified professionals with a government-approved degree or higher qualification in the following fields:Doctors in any branch of Medicine.Individuals with qualifications such as B. Tech / M. Tech / BE / ME / Architect / Engineer.Professionals with qualifications such as CA / ACCA / ICWA / CFA / CS / CFM.Those with degrees such as MCA / MBA / PGDBA / PGDBM LLB/LLM.	Can I convert my existing savings account into a bob Professional Saving Bank Account?Yes, as Bank of Baroda, we offer the option to convert your existing savings account into a Baroda Professional Saving Bank Account. This specialised account is designed to cater to the unique banking needs of professionals. To convert your account, you can visit our nearest branch or contact our customer service for further assistance. Is there a minimum balance requirement for the bob Professional Saving Bank Account?Yes, there is a minimum balance requirement for the Baroda Professional Saving Bank Account. Customers are required to maintain a Minimum Quarterly Average Balance (MQAB) of Rs. 25,000/-. This balance must be maintained on a quarterly basis to avoid any charges or penalties.Is there a fee or charge for opening and maintaining the bob Professional Saving Bank Account?No fees or charges
