It provides various benefits and features specifically tailored to empower women financially. These accounts aim to encourage women to save, invest, and achieve financial independence.What are the benefits of women’s savings account?The benefits of a women's savings account includes a flexi-fixed deposit facility with attractive shopping offers, complementary accounts for children and lower ROI in education loan for girl child, personal accidental insurance, a first-year free RuPay Platinum Debit Card, a discount on two-wheeler loans, and a 25% discount on annual locker rentals. These features provide women with financial security, convenient banking services, and exclusive discounts, making it an advantageous choice for saving and managing their money.	Is there any fees/charges to open women's saving account?There are no fees or charges for opening a women's savings account. However, bank levy charges on the non-maintenance of QAB, SMS alerts (2nd Year onwards.), cheque book (after 30 cheque leafs.) etc.  	How can a housewife open a bob Mahila Shakti saving account?Any woman including a housewife can open a bob Mahila Shakti Saving Account by following simple steps.Visit the nearest Bank of Baroda branch or access their online banking portal.Fill out the account opening form, providing personal details such as name, address, and contact information.Submit the required KYC (Know Your Customer) documents, including identity proof, address proof, and a recent passport-sized photograph.Deposit the minimum required amount specified by the bank to activate the account.Once the documents are verified and the deposit is made, the bob Mahila Shakti Saving Account will be opened, and the account details will be provided to you.What is the eligibility criteria to open a bob Mahila Shakti saving account?To be eligible to open a bob Mahila Shakti Saving Account, you need to be a resident Indian woman who is 18 years of age or older.	What is the minimum balance required to open women's saving account?Account can be opened with any amount, subject to maintenance of QAB as mentioned below.QAB to be maintained:Rs 1000/- RuralRs 2000/- Semi UrbanRs 3000/- Metro/ UrbanHowever, it is advisable to open SB account with minimum balance of QAB as mentioned above to prevent penal charges of the bank.
