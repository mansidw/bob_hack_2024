They are recruited for a service tenure of 4 Years. What are the eligibility criteria to open account under this scheme?Personnel recruited in Indian Army, Indian Air Force, Indian Navy as Agniveer are eligible under this scheme   What is the Agniveer salary package under Agnipath scheme?Personnel recruited under Agnipath scheme of GOI as Agniveers, under Indian Army, Indian Air Force, Indain Navy shall be eligible for PAI cover only after credit of one month salary and thereafter regular salary credit in the account. Since the Agniveers are recruited for a period of 4 Years they will be eligible for benefits for the service tenure only.
