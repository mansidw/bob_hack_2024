An SHGs members deal with similar issues. They assist one another in resolving their issues. SHGs encourage members to make modest financial savings. The savings are kept with the bank.  A self-help group, or SHG for short, is a committee of 12 to 25 local women between the ages of 18 and 50 who act as financial intermediaries.  How do I open a SHG bank account?Self Help Groups, including women SHGs, can open account subject to submission of resolution and list of members of the SHG. Who can open SHG account?Women from impoverished communities can be a part or head an SHG. How does SHG become eligible to take loan from bank?The SHGs must have been operating for at least six months according to their books of accounts and not from the date that their S/B accounts were opened in order to qualify for loans.They should be practise “PANCHSUTRAS” i.e., regular meetings, regular savings, regular inter loaning, timely repayment, and up-to date books of accounts.
